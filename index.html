How it works

Simple for the trader, powerful behind the scenes

The only manual part is defining the framework. After that, Trade Guardian handles the monitoring, interpretation and live guidance automatically.

1

Define your plan

Set account size, funded-account rules, maximum loss, risk per trade, position sizing and session limits.

2

Connect your platform

The system reads trading activity automatically so metrics and behavioral analysis do not depend on manual entry.

3

Trade with your copilot

Trade Guardian compares every action against your plan and alerts you when discipline starts to break down.
